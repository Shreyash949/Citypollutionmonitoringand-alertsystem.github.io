About the System

The City Pollution Awareness and Monitoring System is a comprehensive initiative designed to raise awareness about pollution levels in urban areas and provide actionable insights. The system leverages advanced technology to monitor air and water pollution and suggests strategies to mitigate their adverse effects.

Key Features

Real-Time Monitoring

Track pollution levels across the city using IoT-based sensors.

Data Visualization

Visualize pollution trends through interactive dashboards.

Community Engagement

Engage residents with campaigns and eco-friendly initiatives.
